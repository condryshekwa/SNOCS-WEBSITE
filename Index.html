About SNOCS Group

SNOCS Group combines metallurgical know-how with modern automation and sensing technologies. Our team — experienced in flotation, process control and instrumentation — focuses on improving recovery, reducing reagent consumption and supporting sustainable operations.

Local Expertise

Based in Mpumalanga (Witbank) with regional projects in Joburg and surrounding industrial zones.

Industry Focus

Coal, PGM flotation, mineral processing, environmental water treatment and tailings management.

Services

Process Optimization

Flotation and milling optimization, mass balance analysis and plant performance improvement using both classical engineering and AI-assisted analytics.

Automation & Instrumentation

Control strategies, SCADA/APC integration, predictive maintenance and sensor networks for continuous plant monitoring.

Flotation Vision Systems

Continuous camera-based monitoring of froth bubble size, froth mobility and colour with ML-assisted alerts and integration to APC systems.

Flotation Reagents Supply

Supplying collectors, frothers, depressants and pH modifiers tailored for coal and PGM flotation. We offer product selection, dosing strategies and supply chain management with trusted chemical partners.

Environmental Engineering

Water treatment, tailings optimisation and waste minimisation solutions that lower environmental footprint and improve resource recovery.

Consulting & R&D

Metallurgical flowsheet design, feasibility studies, nitrogen flotation trials and development of Excel add-ins and digital tools to speed engineering calculations.

Technology

We build practical engineering tools: an Excel flowsheet add-in for mass balances, a flotation vision camera unit for continuous monitoring, and dashboards that turn sensor data into actionable insights. We prototype using Python for data pipelines, Java/JavaEE for backend services, and React for frontend dashboards.

Excel Add-in (Engineering Tools)

Custom formulas and shapes for flowsheeting, helping engineers quickly iterate mass balances and equipment sizing.

Data & Analytics

Real-time dashboards, KPI alerts and historical trend analysis to support operational decisions.

Projects & Case Studies

Example projects include nitrogen flotation trials for PGMs, pilot flotation vision system installations, reagent campaigns to reduce collector consumption, and APC integrations for continuous operation.

Pilot: Nitrogen Flotation Trial

Reduced oxidation, improved selectivity and increased concentrate grade in controlled trials.

Reagent Supply Campaign

Optimised dosing and supply logistics for a coal processing plant, resulting in lower operating costs and stable reagent performance.
